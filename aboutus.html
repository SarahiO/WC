About Us


Why make Willow Communications Limited your Audio Visual partner?

Experience

Willow Communications Limited has for over 27 years year provided audio visual services for business and corporate clients.


Focus

We provide only commercial audio visual soultions. We do not sell domestic audio visual equipment or provide lighting solutions.


In House Technical Excellence

We train and manage our engineers in house. This practice enables us to maintain the high levels of expertise and service that we aim for on every project, large or small.


Core Values

We are open and honest and operate as a team with everyone at Willow putting our customers first. We continually learn from our clients and from our projects delivered and always aim to exceed expectations and aim to improve. Our work reflects on the Comapny, not the individual.


Long Term Relationships

We take a long term view to our business relationships and as a consequence have an excellent record of long term client retention.


Assisted Hearing Systems - Action on Hearing Loss


From Bingo Halls to Buckingham Palace, from Boardroom to Meeting Room, willow has installed and maintained assisted hearing systems to improve accessibility and communication. This includes induction loops, speech transfer, infra-red and sound masking systems.

In January 2016, willow Communication was appointed as the sole UK installer for Action for Hearing Loss the UK's leading Charity for confronting deafness, tinnitus and hearing loss.
