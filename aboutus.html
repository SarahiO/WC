About Us


Why make Willow Communications Limited your Audio Visual partner?


Experience

Established in 1989, Willow Communication is an audiovisual installation and integration company working with sound, display technology and digital media.

Whatever the application or budget our goal remains the same, and that is to deliver world class audio visual solutions and environments supported by first class customer service.

We take a long-term view to our business relationships and as a consequence have an excellent record of client satisfaction and retention.

If you are considering starting an audiovisual project, whether large or small, or have an immediate maintenance or service requirement, then we would welcome the opportunity to discuss this with you.


In House Technical Excellence

We train and manage our engineers in house. This practice enables us to maintain the high levels of expertise and service that we aim for on every project, large or small.

Core Values

We are open and honest and operate as a team with everyone at Willow putting our customers first. We continually learn from our clients and from our projects delivered and always aim to exceed expectations and aim to improve. Our work reflects on the Comapny, not the individual.
